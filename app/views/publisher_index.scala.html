Contribute your content to TopicHub

Secondary use of content has created much more complex environment for publishers, where funding mandates, institutional policies, etc often mean making copies (and related metadata) available to a number of parties. Put the content on TopicHub, and all these coordination costs disappear, since it functions as a one-stop location for any consumer of content, and you don't need to track or manage who they are: they interact with the hub, not you.
