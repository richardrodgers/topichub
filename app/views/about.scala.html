TopicHub

TopicHub is a web-based service to facilitate distribution of packaged content, using a publish/subscribe model. Publishers and subscribers are expected to be primarily institutional entities, but the service could also be used by individuals on either end.

The service adheres to open content interoperability standards wherever possible, specifically:

  • Content transfers from publishers to the hub and from the hub to subscribers utilize the SWORD (v1.3) protocol.
  • Metadata for content held by the hub is exposed by an Open Archives Initiative (version 2.0) OAI-PMH data provider endpoint here
  • Content held by the hub may be harvested using the NISO/OAI ResourceSync (draft) protocols.
  • Metadata is also expressed using the Schema.org vocabulary in hub application web pages.
  • Topics identified by the hub are syndicated in Atom feeds.

TopicHub is built upon an open-source, Apache 2 licensed platform available on GitHub.
