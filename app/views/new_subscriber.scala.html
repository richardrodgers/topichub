Define your subscriber profile

You can create your subscriber identity very easily by completing this form. You will then be able to define specific 'destinations' for content you want.

Visibility controls your appearance on the hub - choose 'public' to share your activities.

Choose an accurate name (usually an institution name), since this (and keywords) will be indexed by the hub and be the primary way to discover you. Provide a URL to your site in the link field, and a logo URL if available. Provide a valid contact email address.

Since SWORD is the primary way content is delivered, enter the base URL of the SWORD service your repository runs.

Terms and backfile are optional, but indicate your default instructions when creating new subscriptions

VERY IMPORTANT: when you have completed this form, you must also create one or more 'destinations'. These define where you will want your content or notifications to go. You can define as many desintations as you like. From your subscriber page, just press the 'manage' button.
