Understanding Topics

Topics are at the heart of how TopicHub helps you discover content. Each topic is a simple term belonging to a common vocabulary or name-space, known as its scheme, that describes, characterizes or classifies the article. Topics help organize items into groups that are of interest to subscribers: all articles in a particular journal, everything written by a certain author, etc. Explore the topics within a scheme by clicking on one of the scheme browse links at the left, or search for topics above.
