For Publishers

Want to do your part to enable your authors to comply with Funder and/or Institutional OA mandates? We are a trusted intermediary dedicated to making the flow of content between publishers and repositories easy and friction-free. Deposit your content and metadata once, with TopicHub, and we will do the rest!

For Repositories

Is growth of your IR held back by the operational headaches of identifying and retrieving your faculty's articles? Through TopicHub you can create a content and metadata feed that is customized for your specific Institution. We can notify you of newly published content through email or RSS and, if you choose, we can push the identified OA content directly into your IR using the SWORD protocol.

For Everybody

TopicHub allows you to stay informed on publication trends, or define and track any characteristics that the hub reveals: authors, institutions, and more.
