Explaining TopicHub

TopicHub workflow:

  • Publishers deposit a package containing content and typically related metadata.
  • The hub uses the metadata and content to extract information, and exposes this as a set of topics.
  • Subscribers discover these topics, and register their interest in them on the hub.
  • The hub delivers content to subscribers that match the topics they have identified.
  • When new packages arrive from any publisher, the extraction will often match existing topics.
  • In this case, all subscribers will receive the new package, since they have already subscribed to the topic.
  • Subscribers can thus obtain content without conducting ongoing, repetitive searches for content.
